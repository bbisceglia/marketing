General Questions About MPH@GW

  • How is MPH@GW different from other MPH programs offered by the George Washington University Milken Institute School of Public Health?
    • MPH@GW is an online program, which means that students can attend the program from anywhere in the world.

Students in the program enjoy the benefits of being a GW graduate student, including access to library resources, student services, career services and a global network of well-connected and successful GW alumni.
